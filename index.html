SERVER RULES

Strict enforcement. Read carefully before playing.

01

FailRP / FearRP

You must value your character's life at all times. Comply realistically when held at gunpoint by law enforcement or gangs.

02

RDM & VDM

Random Deathmatch and Vehicle Deathmatch are strictly prohibited. Always have a valid in-character reason before engaging in combat.

03

Metagaming & Powergaming

Using OOC info in-game is strictly forbidden. Forcing impossible RP scenarios onto players without choice will lead to a ban.

04

New Life Rule (NLR)

Upon death/respawn, you forget the events leading to your death. Do not return to the death scene for 15 minutes.

05

Combat Logging

Leaving the game during active RP scenes, police chases, or combat scenarios will result in an instant permanent ban.

06

Respect & OOC Conduct

In-character conflict stays in-character. Toxicity, hate speech, or harassment in OOC chat/voice will get you banned instantly.
